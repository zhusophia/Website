About

Last Updated: May 2023

Hey! I’m Sophia, and I’m so glad you’re browsing SophLand.

Currently, I’m a student at the University of Waterloo and Wilfrid Laurier University, concurrently pursuing a double degree in Mathematics and Business Administration. I have experience with stuff, and two cats!

My two founding principles are curiosity and vivacity. I’m constantly learning and diving into niche rabbit holes. I’m also a big believer in excitement, and I always aspire to inject positivity into my environment. I tried to capture a bit of it in SophLand: You need to explore and click around to enter, after all.

I have three guiding goals:

  • Learn about interesting topics so I have a wide arsenal of tools
  • Building things to positively impact the communities I'm apart of
  • Have fun :D

Also, a smattering of (other) interests before you leave:

  • Rolling a coin over my knuckles
  • Documentaries
  • Case competitions
  • Jiujitsu / Gym
  • Photography
  • Books / Manga

Enjoy your stay at SophLand!
