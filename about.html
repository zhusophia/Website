About

Last Updated: May 2023

Hey! I’m Sophia, and I’m so glad you’re browsing SophLand.

Currently, I’m a student at the University of Waterloo and Wilfrid Laurier University, concurrently pursuing a BMath/BBA double degree. I have experience with stuff, and two cats!

My two founding principles are curiosity and vivacity. I’m constantly learning about random facts and diving into a niche rabbit hole. I’m a big believer in excitement, and I always aspire to inject positivity into my environment. I tried to capture a bit of it in SophLand: You need to explore and click around to enter, after all.

I have three guiding goals:
  • Learn about interesting topics so I have a wide arsenal of tools
  • Building things to positively impact the communities I'm apart of
  • Have fun :D

Also, a smattering of (other) interests before you leave:
  • Rolling a coin over my knuckles
  • YouTube documentaries
  • Case competitions
  • Jiujitsu / Gym
  • Photography
  • Books / Manga

Enjoy your stay at SophLand!
