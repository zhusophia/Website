Role

Pog Networks helps Twitch streamers connect through 50+ weekly online competitions and socials.

My friends and I started Pog Networks in the midst of the pandemic, when isolation was at its highest. During this time, we realized that many flocked to live-streaming services such as Twitch to meet people and play games with others. However, these streamers’ aspirations were quickly destroyed, as many struggled to meet other streamers and had to compete for viewers. The connection they longed for and the communities they wanted to build… didn’t exist?

That’s where Pog steps in. We’re here to make this process easier for streamers as we pair them up in duos and host 3-hour long events to build connections--all while streamers chilling with their chat and playing their favourite game!

It wasn’t easy. Our first tournament was rough. Nothing was efficient and we made so many mistakes. But overtime, we got a lot better! We built a match-making system, taking a streamer’s skill, preferences, profanity and more into considerations when choosing teams! We started streaming ourselves to give streamers opportunities to commentate when they weren’t playing. We even expanded our team and trained social media managers to help us with marketing!

Ultimately, we closed because our operations became too manual and took a lot of time away from our academics. However, I’ve learnt so much as I juggled dozens of hats while building Pog up from scratch.
