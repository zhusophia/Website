Adelia Elle

Social Media Manager

Website

2020 - 2021

Social media marketing, data analysis, SEO, product management

Role

Adelia Elle (Adelia) is a skincare e-commerce, selling luxurious and sustainable beauty-tech. Let us take care of your skin. Working at Adelia perfectly combined my interests in tech and beauty!

Here, I focused on socia media marketing (Facebook Ads), social media management (mainly Instagram, Facebook and Pinterest), and creating our brand ambassador program. I also completed other tasks, such as setting up our mailing list and creating weekly data analysis presentations to showcase my findings to the CEO.

I think that my desire for meaningful and varied tasks, as well as my independent work style stem from Adelia. Due to the nature of the small company (it was just the CEO and I), I was able to try out so many different roles and (nearly) every contribution I made was implemented. Furthermore, I was typically given vague instructions, where I was told to do what I saw fit. It was high impact work coupled with lots of freedom. I felt a strong sense of responsibility , while being entertained by the numerous roles I filled. Working here was incredibly enjoyable!

The most important thing Adelia taught me was “understanding data”. When dealing with social media algorithms and marketing optimization, data is king. I spent hours researching “best practices” and analyzing trends to see which Facebook Ads work best. Slowly built hashtag sets to ensure our Instagram posts had the best reach. I’m adamant: Marketing is a numbers game and I need to win.
