Details

Adelia Elle (Adelia) is a skincare e-commerce, selling luxurious and sustainable beauty tech. It was the first official job I have ever had, and I remember being ecstatic when I got the job!

Working at Adelia has definitely shaped a lot of my work preferences. For example, my desire for varied tasks, as well as my independent work style. This is because Adelia was a small company (it was just the CEO and I), so it meant that I was able to try out every role and wore lots of hats! Furthermore, I was typically given vague instructions and was told to do what I saw fit. This environment resulted in high-impact work coupled with lots of freedom and responsibility—the perfect trio.

December 2020: Social Media

Every single day, I would engage as many accounts on Instagram as possible, trying to establish ourselves within the industry. I learned the art of writing Instagram comments, building hashtag sets, and planning our profile—nothing would be left behind

March 2021: Brand Ambassadors

I decided to start a brand ambassador campaign to promote the launch of our newest product! I reached out to influencers with similar audiences, drafted a contract, and built an Influencer Kit for photo inspiration and to communicate our values! Alongside that, I managed our monthly emails, building templates to give subscribers secret coupons and behind-the-scenes to our photo shoots. Truly the peak of customer relationship management.

May 2021: Facebook Ads

I was given a chance to experience using Facebook Ads to market our products. It was here where I learned how to “understand data”—the most important lesson. I spent hours researching “best practices” and analyzing trends to see which Facebook Ads work best. Analyzing results after the promotion period. Iterating on designs to marginally improve performance.
I always ended each week presenting my findings to the CEO and discussing what I should do in the future. My weekly experiments are something I look back on fondly; Data is so fun to play with :)
